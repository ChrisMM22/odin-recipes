Tuna Omelette!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pot of tuna
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ chopped tomato
  • ¼ chopped red pepper
  • 1 chopped spring onion
  • ¼ cup of chopped corriander
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Chilli flakes(optional)
  • 2 heaped talespoon of flour
  • 2 tablespoon of sunflower oil

Instructions:

  1. Put tomatoes,red pepper, spring onion and corriander into a bowl

  2. Add the tuna into the bowl and break down into small pieces

  3. Add the 3 eggs into the bowl

  4. Add salt, ground pepper, and chilli flakes(optional) to your taste

  5. Add the 2 heaped tablespoons of flour

  6. Mix all the contents with a spoon thoroughly

  7. Prep the pan, add the oil and let it heat for 20-30

  8. Pour the contents of the bowl into the pan and spread evenly around the ban

  9. Let the omlette cook on one side on low-medium heat, flip once that side has reached a golden colour(about 3-4 mins)

  10. Do the same for the other side and voilà!
